Mahendra Multiple Campus Nepalgunj

Mahendra Multiple Campus, Nepalgunj is a public co-educational institution located in Nepalgunj city in western Nepal. It is one of the campuses affiliated to the Tribhuvan University. It is named after the King Mahendra. The campus was established in 2014 BS as Narayan Inter College.
